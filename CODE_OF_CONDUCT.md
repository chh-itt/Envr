# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We as maintainers and contributors pledge to make participation in the `envr` project and community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- being respectful of differing opinions, experiences, and technical approaches
- giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- focusing on what is best for the project and its users
- showing empathy toward other community members
- assuming good intent while still discussing problems clearly and directly

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- the use of sexualized language or imagery, and sexual attention or advances of any kind
- trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- public or private harassment
- publishing others' private information, such as a physical or email address, without their explicit permission
- other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable behavior. Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, documentation, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct, and will communicate reasons for moderation decisions when appropriate.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces, including issue trackers, pull requests, discussions, release notes, and other public-facing community channels. It also applies when an individual is officially representing the project in public spaces.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, or have other community conduct concerns, contact the maintainers through the support and security channels documented in [`SUPPORT.md`](SUPPORT.md) and [`SECURITY.md`](SECURITY.md). If the concern involves a sensitive safety or harassment issue, prefer a private channel instead of a public issue.

Maintainers will review and investigate reports promptly and fairly, and will respect the privacy and security of the reporter to the extent reasonably possible.

## Enforcement guidelines

Maintainers may take any action they deem appropriate in response to unacceptable behavior, including:

- warning the offender
- deleting or editing content
- temporarily locking threads or limiting interaction
- temporarily or permanently banning a participant from project spaces
